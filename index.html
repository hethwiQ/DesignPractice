What is NeuBrutalism?

NeuBrutalism stand's out as something distinct and unconventional when contrasted with the prevalent flat designs of today. The stark contrast between contemporary design and the aesthetics of the pre-2000s era is especially noticeable to younger generations who didn't experience the design sensibilities of that earlier period. This creates an interesting foundation for the emergence of a new trend.

 Key Characteristics:

  • Unusual background color theme
  • Lack of gradients
  • Bold modern and futuristic typography
  • Static animations
  • Basic shapes
  • Hard contrasts
  • Dark outlines
  • Hard shadows (45 degree shadows that are the same color and opacity as the outline)
  • Random and inconsistent layout
